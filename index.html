Hello! I’m Madhumitha, an enthusiastic professional with a passion for working with data.

I am currently pursuing my master’s in information systems at Iowa State University in the United States. I had the wonderful opportunity of working as a graduate teaching assistant in the Computer Science department. I taught students to use Microsoft Excel spreadsheets and Microsoft Access databases to input, store, process, manipulate, query, and analyze data for business and industrial applications.

Before coming to the United States, I worked at Larsen and Toubro Infotech. I spent three years honing my skills and advancing from a graduate engineer trainee to a senior data engineer. My main roles were as an ETL developer and a SQL developer. As an ETL developer, I extracted data from numerous sources, transformed it as per the business requirements, and loaded it into the data warehouse. As a SQL developer, I used queries to create procedures and triggers, as well as for creating and managing data and databases. I implemented multiple units from scratch, from requirement gathering and development to documentation. I collaborated with analysts, managers, and clients on a day-to-day basis to develop analytical solutions. I was honored with an "Outstanding Performer" rating for two consecutive years.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I find solace in the pages of books. The Harry Potter series holds a special place in my heart as an all-time favorite. Additionally, I enjoy immersing myself in the world of movies, and I have recently embarked on a journey into the realm of digital painting.

I am eager to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to make a meaningful impact in the ever-evolving field of data.
